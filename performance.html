performance

Life is a performance. Society is our stage. And our audience? The neoliberal social order. We can choose who we present ourselves to be, so long as it satisfies our audience. But our audience is quite picky. If they are not satisfied, they pluck us from the stage and wait for better actors to take our places. The self does not belong to us. The self is a commodity. The self is a brand.

As such, there is no room for authenticity.
As such, there is no room for failure.

resistance

Indeed, the neoliberal world seeks to commodify us. Pit us against each other, singing false promises of success for the victors. But we, the actors, the runners in this race, are waking up. More and more, we realize that the course neoliberalism seeks to set for our lives is not the only path available. In fact, it’s not even the best one. If we want life to be less daunting, this is a truth we must accept.

Accept　it, then act on it.

But for all your talk of resistance, tell me this. What could one person do? What could any of us do? The price of rejecting society is that society rejects you back. It couldn’t care less.

So how do we resist in a way that matters?

Resistance is not all-or-nothing. It does not have to be an explosion, a violent battle cry, an outright declaration of war. Most importantly, the price of resistance is not a price one must bear alone.

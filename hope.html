hope

Of course I still fear the future. Things change so fast these days, and not always for the better.

But, a future of slowness and shared love…that does sound quite comforting. And I’m sure I’m not the only one who thinks so. Perhaps, then, the first step in my own resistance, is to have faith in the future.

To trust, despite all uncertainty, that we will choose to slow down and care for one another on this long, arduous journey.

So, I don't think you need to worry. It will all work out. You'll see.

After all, I'm still here.
